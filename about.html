Dubbed 'The Sparkling Diamond of Burlesque', Belle de Beauvoir is an award-winning powerhouse performer that both allures and challenges her audience. Combining the tantalising tease of the famed Montmartre courtesans with the brazen bump ’n’ grind of a fifties pin-up girl, Belle tours internationally, gracing stages across Europe with her own sultry brand of classic showgirl burlesque.

Following a sold-out five-star Edinburgh Fringe run, Belle has recently performed on London’s West End where she was heralded as ‘personifying the genre’ of burlesque. She is currently on a UK Tour with the longest running burlesque show in Europe, An Evening of Burlesque. Belle is a featured model in Bombshell and RetroLovely magazines as well as being a writer for SheRose Magazine on the art of burlesque. As CEO of Prima Donna Ltd, Belle produces sell-out successes such as Blues and Burlesque, Big Band Burlesque, Soho Cabaret Club and Prima Dona Cabaret and has taught burlesque for the legendary Pineapple Dance Studios.
